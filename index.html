About Me

I was born in Dallas, TX, but grew up in Richmond, VA. I graduated from George Mason University in 2010 with a degree in economics. I currently live in Arlington, VA. I work as a QA Analyst for United States Citizenship and Immigration Services (USCIS).

In my free time, I try to stay as active as possible. You can find me in the gym 4-5 days a week. I'm a huge sports junkie, and like to both watch and play sports. As you can guess, I'm a Dallas Cowboys fan.

Church is also a huge part of my life. I attend Grace Community Church. I've recently become really involved, serving on a number of different leadership teams as well as volunteering on Sundays.
